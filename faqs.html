Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to your questions about our AI-powered virtual try-on platform, designed for visualizing any fashion style from any source.

What is the virtual try-on platform?

Our platform uses advanced AI and AR technology to let you virtually try on any type of clothing or accessory. Upload a photo or use your live camera to see how outfits look on you in real-time. It’s designed exclusively for fashion visualization, not for purchasing items.

Can I try on any type of clothing or accessory?

Yes! Our platform supports all types of wear, including dresses, tops, pants, jackets, accessories, and more. There are no restrictions on categories, so you can try any fashion item you choose.

Can I upload outfits from any source?

Absolutely! You can upload images of outfits or accessories from any source, such as your personal wardrobe, online stores, designer catalogs, or social media. Our AI will overlay the item onto your photo or live camera feed with high accuracy.

Is this platform for shopping?

No, our platform is strictly for virtual try-on. It allows you to visualize how outfits look on you without any shopping functionality. You can experiment with styles from any source to make informed fashion decisions.

How do I use the virtual try-on feature?

It’s easy: upload a photo of yourself or use your device’s camera for a live capture. Then, upload an image of the outfit or accessory you want to try. Our AI will seamlessly overlay the item on your image, letting you explore different looks in real-time.

What devices are supported?

Our platform is compatible with any device running a modern web browser, including smartphones, tablets, and desktops. For the live camera feature, you’ll need a device with a camera and browser permission to access it.

How accurate are the try-on previews?

Our AI-driven technology delivers photorealistic previews that accurately reflect fit and style. The quality of the preview depends on the image quality, but our system is optimized for precise and lifelike visualizations.

Do I need to download an app to use the platform?

No, our platform is entirely web-based, so you can access it directly from your browser without downloading any software.
